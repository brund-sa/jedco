About Us

Who we are

Jizan International Development Company (JEDCO) is a Saudi company, managed by highly qualified Saudi youth whose aim is to innovate in customer service and to develop and promote land transport. It is one of the most important activities of GEDCO, which relies on a fleet of trucks and trailers owned by the company and equipped with the latest technology tracking systems in Saudi Arabia, and the company depends on the work of transport speed, accuracy and punctuality.

JEDCO focuses its services on providing logistics solutions to its customers in a manner that achieves their satisfaction and efficiency. The management of the company consists of a highly qualified and qualified team to provide and provide services to the company's clients in a way that satisfies their satisfaction. The members of the management team have extensive experience in the field of transport and logistics.
